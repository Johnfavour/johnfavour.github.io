About Section

My name is Chukwuemeka Favour Mesoma
The name of my music website is Egonic,
and what inspired me to start a music project
was my love for music. The name of my music website
was inspired by the fusion of two powerful elements, which is;
Individuality and sonic expression.
'Ego' represents the confidence and authenticity we encourage
artists to embrace as they create and share their unique sounds.
The term 'Egonic' draws from the word 'sonic', emphasizing the focus
on sound and the auditory experience that lies at the heart of our platform.
Through our platform, we invite you to explore the limitless possibilities of sound, immerse yourself in
transformative melodies, and uncover the magic that lies within the intersection of Ego and Sonic.
